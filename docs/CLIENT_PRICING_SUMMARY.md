## FAQ

**Q: Why is this so cheap?**
A: We use free AI models when possible. When we need premium models, we pass the cost through at zero markup. You only pay the 30% service fee for quality review and file management.

**Q: Can I really get a website for $301?**
A: Yes. The setup is $299. The AI tasks to build it cost about $2.24. Compare that to $2,500 from an agency.

**Q: What if the AI does bad work?**
A: Every deliverable goes through quality review. If it's not good, we retry with a better model at our cost, not yours.

**Q: Do I own my files?**
A: 100%. Everything lives in YOUR Google Drive. We can't access it after you revoke permissions.

**Q: What's the catch?**
A: There's no catch. We're just really efficient at using AI. The hard part (strategy, quality review) still requires a human (that's us, and we make our money on the $299 setup + 30% service fee).
