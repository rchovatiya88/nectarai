## FAQ

**Q: Why so cheap? What's the catch?**
A: No catch. Modern free AI models (Llama, DeepSeek) are as capable as paid models from 2 years ago. We route your task to the cheapest model that can do the job. Most tasks cost us $0.00. You pay $0.00.

**Q: What if I want premium quality?**
A: Add "use premium" to your request. We'll use Claude Opus or GPT-5. Cost is still <$5 per task. You see the estimate before confirming.

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT Plus?**
A: ChatGPT is a tool. You write prompts. We are a workforce. You say "build me a website" and Bees do everything — design, code, content, deploy. No learning curve.

**Q: Can I really cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. No contracts. No minimums. Delete the Telegram bot and your access is gone. Your files stay in your Google Drive.
