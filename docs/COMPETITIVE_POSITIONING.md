## Objection Handling

**"This sounds too cheap. What's the catch?"**
> There's no catch. AI models are now free (Llama, DeepSeek) or nearly free. We don't have salaries, offices, or benefits to pay. The 30% service fee covers orchestration and quality review. Traditional agencies charge 10–20x because they have humans doing everything.

**"Can AI really do quality work?"**
> For 80% of business tasks, yes. Free models today are as good as GPT-3.5 was in 2023. For the 20% that needs premium quality (complex code, creative strategy), we use Claude Opus or GPT-5. You see the model choice and can override it.

**"What if I need something custom?"**
> That's what DevBee and MLBee are for. We build custom tools, fine-tune models, and create bespoke workflows. Custom work is quoted upfront, not billed hourly.

**"How is this different from just using ChatGPT?"**
> ChatGPT is a tool. You write prompts, manage context, format output, and do the integration. NECTAR.AI is a workforce. You say "build me a website" and 6 Bees coordinate to deliver a live URL — design, code, content, deploy, and QA.

**"What about data privacy?"**
> Your data never trains public models. We use isolated environments. Google Drive files are yours — we get writer access, you can revoke anytime. SOC 2 and GDPR compliance available on enterprise tier.

**"What if the AI makes a mistake?"**
> Every Bee has confidence thresholds. If output is ambiguous or high-stakes, it flags for review. You set autonomy: from "draft everything for approval" to "handle it completely." Plus 2 free revisions on every deliverable.
